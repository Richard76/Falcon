Missing Value Treatment

The variables Customer Type, Type Travel, Departure Arrival Time Convienent, Food_drink, and onboard service all had between about 8% and 10% missing values. As they are all factors variables, I converted the missing values to a new factor level called "Unknown".

The variable ArrivalDelay_Mins had about 284 or 0.31% missing values. Rather than just remove the missing values I created a new column called ArrivalDelay_NA and made it 1 if there was an NA there then replaced the NA's with 0 in the ArrivalDelayin_Mins colmn. It may turn out that a missing value here is a good predictor of satisfaction.

Outlier treatment

Departure Delay in Mins and Arrival Delay in Mins both have outliers. The issue can be minimized by creating a new column by talking the log of each original column but I am keeping the original columns at least for a bit so I can use them for feature engineering before I delete them later.

Analytical Approach (2)

Alternative analytical approaches that could have beeen applied to the problem

This whole approach assumes that we should change based upon what our current customers think of us. This is probably the same approach that every other airline is doing. Its a relatively safe bet that most of the other airlines are probably doing or attempting to do as we speak. It probably wont jump out as genius in 2020.

An alternate approach would be to find out which segments of customers are really happy with us and spend all our resources on targeting more people like them (assuming there are lots more of them around) and changing who we are just to make them happier. That could mean driving away the segments of customers that only kind of like us in an attempt to replace them with the segments of customers that really like us. It's a risky alternative approach but hey, half the airlines are probably going to go bankrupt over the next few years anyway. If we do what our competitors are doing then we will get the same results, a 50% chance of going bankrupt. If the approach doesn't work then the airline may fail (as expected) but if it works, we did something that changes the game.
